Greek Pasta Salad

This is a great make-ahead pasta salad loaded with the flavors of Greece!

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Red Wine Vinegar
  • Garlic Powder
  • Dried Basil
  1. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, garlic powder, basil, oregano, black pepper, and sugar together in a large bowl; add cooked pasta, mushrooms, tomatoes, red peppers, feta cheese, green onions, olives, and pepperoni. Toss until evenly coated. Cover, and chill 2 hours or overnight.
